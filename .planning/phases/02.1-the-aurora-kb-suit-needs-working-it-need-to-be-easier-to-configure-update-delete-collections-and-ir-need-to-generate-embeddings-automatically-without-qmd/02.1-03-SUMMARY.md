## Decisions Made
- Enforced lock acquisition at service entry for every mutating operation so scheduler and manual flows share one exact concurrency boundary.
- Persisted `last_planned_slot_utc` even on failed due-run execution to preserve single catch-up semantics and prevent repeated retries for the same missed slot.
- Rendered scheduler status with UTC `Z` timestamps in both text and JSON to keep outputs deterministic and parse-friendly.
